I started this company in 2020 as a backup plan for myself when the National Parks shut down. I had been working in the Grand Canyon and was laid off due to the pandemic. I have since done several weddings, family portraits, maternity shoots, engagements, album covers, corporate retreats, graduation shoots, and multiple local events.
