Code Buddy Discord Bot

I designed Code-Buddy to help get myself ready for coding interviews. This Bot provides random questions and code challenges based on a chosen subject and difficulty level. To use Code-Buddy, type "code-buddy" and a subject ("React", "CSS", "JavaScript" etc.) and a difficulty level ("1/10", "7/10", etc.). Type "answer" to get the answer to the previous question.

Lane G Photography

I began offering my photography services for hire in 2020 as a backup plan for myself when the National Parks shut down. At the time, I had been working in the Grand Canyon and was laid off due to the pandemic. As a hobby photography for over ten years, I decided I would to try to make a living at it. I built a simple website, started a social media presence, and began marketing myself. I have since done several weddings, family portraits, maternity shoots, engagements, album covers, corporate retreats, graduation shoots, and multiple local events. I have been able to automate most of the business, and it is now a fairly passive source of income, with the exception of showing up for an hour or two here and there.
